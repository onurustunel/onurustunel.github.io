I write about the engineering decisions behind products.

I'm interested in how technical choices shape product outcomes, and how the wrong decisions quietly kill products before they ever reach users.

This blog is where I explore product ideas, analyze real systems, and break down the tradeoffs behind building software.

Not from a purely technical perspective, but from the intersection of engineering and product thinking.
